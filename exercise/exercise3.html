UI Evaluation of Vanier Interactive Map

Exercise 1.3

Description

For this assignment, my team and I reviewed the Vanier Interactive map. Overall the map has great colors and is very descriptive. There are color coded sections that align with different sections of the college. The appearance is modern and laid out well.Overall the map has a good visually appealing layout, but could use a few touch ups.

Concerns

  • Clickability: Some sections are clickable but they do not go anywhere.
  • Finding rooms: Rooms seem to not exist on the map, and well as buildings.
  • Consistency: If you are in a private tab, you are able to get the full experience of the map. But on mobile device and a regular browser, the map maybe interactive and sometimes not.
  • Updates: The map has not been updated in 2 years.

Improvements

  • Clickability: Make every click count. Leading the user to the specified location.
  • Finding rooms: A user should not have to be a detective to locate a room.
  • Consistency: The map should be available and interactive on all devices.
  • Updates: A dedicated person or team, should keep the map updated.
