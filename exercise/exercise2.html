UX Evaluation of Kitchen Drawer

Exercise 1.2

Let's talk about those handy kitchen drawers. They're not very exciting and only have one purpose, to open and close the drawer. It does its job very well for the most part.

  • Accessibility: Easy to open and close.
  • Operatoration: Getting what you need quickly and effortlessly.
  • Durable: Quality materials and great craftsmanship.
  • Safety:Rounded edges, soft-close, and locks.

They are accessible, smooth operation, durability, But I think it coukd be better.

Concerns

  • Germs: everyone touches them and never thinks of what is left behind
  • Shape: it is possible to be injured by the design of the drawer.
  • Apperance: Unless you have a specific design, they mostly look the same.

Improvements

  • Germs: a drawer that has a layer of antiseptic layer that disinfects the drawer after being handled.
  • Shape: A drawer with no handle, and works by waving your hand across it.
  • Apperance: Seemless and intergrated into the cabiniet design.
